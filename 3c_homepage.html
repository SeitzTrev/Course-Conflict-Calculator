Welcome to 3C!

3C is a faculty tool which will help professors eliminate class conflicts for students, such as having classes which are co-requisite scheduled at the same time. The intention is to give students a smoother college career, rather than needing to wait to take certain requisites.

What you will need:

Faculty members need to upload a spreadsheet of classes they plan to teach to the 3C calculator. This is done on the 3C Calc page.
